This CGPA tool is for students in any tertitary institution that uses the 5.0 scale. That is, any student in any part of the world can use it. Please also note that you need to make sure of your scoring system. For this CGPA calculator to work for you, the following have to apply.

  • The F (Fail) grade must be bewteen 0 and 39.
  • E for your school must be between 40 and 44.
  • D is graded as scores from 45 to 49.
  • C is any score bewteen 50 and 59
  • B is a score from 60 to 69, and finally,
  • A is any score from 70 to 100 marks.
